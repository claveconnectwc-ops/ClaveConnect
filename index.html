Before you reach out
What to expect when you work with us

We want to be fully transparent about who we are and how we work, so there are no surprises.

⚠️

We are not attorneys

Clave Connect is a concierge and matching service. We do not give legal advice, represent you in court, or practice law. What we do is find you the right licensed professional and make sure you arrive prepared.

💵

Two separate fees

You pay Clave Connect for our matching and preparation service. Your attorney or paralegal charges their own fees separately. We will always be upfront about what each attorney charges before you commit.

⏱️

What happens after you contact us

You reach out, we respond within 24 hours. We listen to your situation, recommend the right plan, and once you confirm we get to work right away. No long waits, no confusing paperwork on your end.

🤝

We are here for you, not the attorney

We work for you. Our goal is to make sure you feel confident and informed throughout your legal process. If something is not working, we will tell you and help you find a better option.
